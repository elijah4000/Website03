Nirmal Purja

Nirmal Purja is without a doubt the best or at least most accomplished high altitude alpinist. Growing up in the himalayan region of nepal Nirmal (nims) grew up around the tallest mountains in the world and had climbed every 8000 metre peak befroe his 30th birthday. However this would not be the greatest of his achivements. He was not only opne of the first people to climb all of the 8000 metre peaks in the world he is also the fastest. In 2014 He became the first person to climb all 14 peaks in under a year completing this feat in only seven months.
